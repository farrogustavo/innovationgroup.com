Our Story

Built on Friendship, Trust, and Excellence

Innovation Group Projects LLC was founded by Antonio and Rumen, lifelong friends who first met while studying Mechanical Maintenance Engineering in Venezuela more than 20 years ago.

Throughout the years, they shared the same passion for engineering, problem-solving, and building solutions that improve people's lives. After gaining valuable experience in industrial, maintenance, and construction environments, they recognized an opportunity to create something of their own.

In 2020, during the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, Innovation Group Projects LLC was born with a clear purpose: to provide homeowners, businesses, and contractors with reliable, safe, and high-quality electrical solutions they could trust.

Today, the company proudly serves commercial, industrial, and residential clients throughout Tennessee, helping bring projects to life while maintaining high standards of safety, workmanship, and customer satisfaction.

Est. 2020

At Innovation Group Projects, every project is more than a job. It is an opportunity to build lasting relationships through trust, quality, and performance.
