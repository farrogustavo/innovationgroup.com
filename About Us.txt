About Us

Built on Friendship, Trust, and Excellence

Innovation Group Projects LLC was founded by Antonio and Rumen, lifelong friends who first met while studying Mechanical Maintenance Engineering in Venezuela more than 20 years ago.

Throughout the years, we shared the same passion for engineering, problem-solving, and building solutions that improve people’s lives. After gaining valuable experience in the industrial, maintenance, and construction industries, we recognized an opportunity to create something of our own.

In 2020, during the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, Innovation Group Projects LLC was born with a clear purpose: to provide homeowners, businesses, and contractors with reliable, safe, and high-quality electrical solutions they could trust.

What began as an idea between two friends quickly evolved into a company committed to delivering professional electrical services with integrity, technical excellence, and attention to detail.

Today, we proudly serve commercial, industrial, and residential clients throughout Tennessee, helping bring projects to life while maintaining the highest standards of safety, workmanship, and customer satisfaction.

At Innovation Group Projects, every project is more than a job—it is an opportunity to build lasting relationships through trust, quality, and performance.

We are the ones you can trust.
